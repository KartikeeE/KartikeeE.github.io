Socio-economic determinants of health in India

An interactive analysis of how economic development, health system capacity, and basic services relate to long-run health outcomes in India.

Home Portfolio Project

Aims

This project examines how changes in India’s life expectancy are associated with key socio-economic and health system indicators over time.

Data

Data are sourced from World Bank/WHO indicators (via World Bank Data360 and WDI). Unit of analysis: India, country-year.
